Biography

Early Life

Praveen Singh was born on October 1, 1975, in Sitapur, Uttar Pradesh. He grew up in a humble household, where he learned the values of hard work, integrity, and compassion from his parents.

Education

Praveen attended Lucknow University where he pursued his education. His academic excellence and leadership skills set the foundation for his future endeavors in public service.

Political Career

Praveen embarked on his political journey in 2009 when he joined the Samajwadi Party. In 2011, he was elected as the Block Pramukh of Machrehta in Sitapur. In March 2024, Praveen joined the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), furthering his commitment to nation-building.

Community Engagement

Aside from his political career, Praveen is actively involved in community service initiatives. He has spearheaded several campaigns aimed at addressing social issues such as education, healthcare, and environmental conservation.
